Welcome to the
Roar Freak Club.

4444 Roar Freak

The Roar Freak Club is a collection of 4444 Roar Freak NFTs inspired by the new wealthy generation of crypto-currency and NFTs. These digital collectibles are established in the Solanaverse.

Each piece is a unique artwork with a collection of more than 150+ traits. The objective is to build the strongest community and project around NFTs.

Roadmap

Here is what we have planned for this project.

01

1st Phase

Some of Roar Freak NFTs will be airdropped to our early adopters and fanbase.

We ramp up our Discord and social media management, which will include a pack of community managers and moderators to bring our Roar Freak community to the moon 🌙

02

2nd Phase

$20,000 donation fund will be established and with the help of the community we will decide on a charity who is a good partner for our project.

With the project we want to bring together a community of Roar Freak.

03

3rd Phase

An exclusive merch line will drop.

This will be hats, Tshirts, and most importantly apparel for your Roar Freak!

We already have several designs done and ready to go!

04

4th Phase

Community grant fund of $30,000 is launched. Create, design, develop, or build something that the community values (e.g. extra utility for the Roar Freak, additional art, memes, etc.) and receive funding from team. In addition to this 75% of the royalty fees will go into the community grant forever! Building out the longevity and community is our #1 priority.

05

5th Phase

We will establish a liquidity pool and seed it in order to help stabilize the price of the Roar Freak NFTs/token. The plan is to launch this 2-3 days after launch and buy up a handful of doges at floor price. Companion NFT will be dropped. (Every Roar Freak lover that is holding a Roar Freak down the road will get a companion NFT for free)
